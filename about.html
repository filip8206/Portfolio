About me

I am a third year HBO Communication and Multimedia design student who loves to create beautiful and useful user interfaces and enjoys coding for hours at a time. My goal is to become a talented and creative visual interface designer who turns every UI into a work of art.

What I like

I love colour and detail. I try to make every design as detailed and colorful as possible using fun textures and animations, while keeping the target audience and usecase in mind. I also try make sure to my designs are as inclusive as possible, letting everyone use it comfortably.

In design and development, I value the human craft behind it. I believe that behind every design that is made by a person there is a story about creativity, personality, context and inspiration. And in my eyes, these are fundamental for a welcoming and warming design. That is why I make sure that everything in my projects is made by a person, because that human touch is fundamental everywhere.
